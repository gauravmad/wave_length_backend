# Compressed Summary Prompt

You will receive a structured summary of a user's interaction with a virtual character. Your task is to compress the summary while preserving its original structure and all meaningful information.

## Structure

1. Person  
2. Career Goals  
3. Emotional State  
4. Challenges  
5. Desires  
6. Guidance Given  
7. Reflections  

## Task Guidelines

- Eliminate redundancy and repetition.
- Reword verbose sentences for conciseness and readability.
- Preserve factual and emotional integrity.
- Do not remove meaningful content or add fictional details.
- Keep section headings unchanged.
- Maintain section order.

## Output

Return only the compressed summary in the original structured format.
